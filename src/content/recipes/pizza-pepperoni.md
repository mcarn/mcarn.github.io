# Ingredients

## Pooling

- Flour 300g
- Water 300g
- Instant yeast sachet

## Pizza Dough

- Flour 330g
- Water 70g
- Salt 14g

## Toppings

- Mozarella cheese
- Pepperoni
- Passata tomato sauce

# Instructions

## Pooling

1. Make the poolish – mix 300g water and 300g flour with 0.6g dried yeast in a large mixing bowl.
2. Cover tightly (with cling film or plastic bag) and leave to prove for around 10 hours at room temperature (or until the poolish is really frothy with lots of bubbles).

## Pizza Dough

1. Make the dough – mix 70g water, 330g flour, and 14g salt into poolish to form a large pizza dough.
2. Cover and rest for 10 minutes to 1 hour.
3. Knead dough for 5-10 minutes (or until dough is stretchy and smooth).
4. Cover and rest for 10 minutes to 1 hour.
5. Divide and shape dough into 4 equal dough balls.
6. Cover and prove for around 8-10 hours at room temperature (or until doubled in size with small bubbles).
7. Shape.

## Cooking

1. Pre-heat the oven to 200C + fan
2. Cover with mozarella.
3. Add pepperoni
4. Put in the oven for 20 to 30min
